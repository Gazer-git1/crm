Mortgage partnership program

Partner banks may invite Lease-to-Own customers to conduct all program payments through their bank throughout the Lease-to-Own term.

This allows the bank to build a long-term relationship and gain visibility into the customer's financial behavior years before the mortgage application.

In return, participating banks may provide customers with a Mortgage Partnership Letter confirming that, subject to the bank's lending policies and the customer's continued eligibility, the bank intends to give priority consideration to the customer's future mortgage application.

What the bank receives

A long-term banking relationship
Monthly payment flows throughout the program
Verified income history
Documented payment discipline
A customer with accumulated Purchase Option Balance

What the customer receives
