Asset-Backed Credit Linked Notes

Instead of purchasing property directly, you purchase an Asset-Backed Credit Linked Note (CLN).

Every CLN finances one specific home.

Every CLN is backed by:

  • one residential property;
  • one registered Lease-to-Own agreement;

Investment Protection

Your investment is protected by multiple layers.

Underlying Property

Every CLN is secured by ownership of one specific residential property.

Purchase Option Balance

Every monthly payment increases the customer's Purchase Option Balance. This represents the customer's growing financial commitment to completing the program.

Customer Commitment Ratio

As the Purchase Option Balance increases, the Customer Commitment Ratio also increases. The more the customer has invested, the lower the probability of voluntary default.

Market Appreciation

The property itself may increase in value during the Lease-to-Own program, creating an additional layer of protection.

Predefined Exit Strategies

Every CLN includes a predefined investment policy before issuance. No decisions are made under pressure. Every scenario is agreed in advance.

Every investor receives access to real-time information.

Option A

Transfer the property to a new qualified Lease-to-Own customer.

The new customer receives a predefined Purchase Option Balance, allowing the investment program to continue.

Option B

Sell the property.

The property may be sold using a discount strategy defined by the Investment Policy, while seeking to preserve investor returns.

Option C

Continue under revised strategy.

Continue operating the property under a revised investment strategy, if this is expected to maximize long-term investor value.
